# Sustainability Performance and Firm Resilience During Crises

This repository contains and materials related to the paper that investigates whether firms with better sustainability performance are more resilient during crises.

## Paper

- **Title:** Are firms with better sustainability performance more resilient during crises  
- **Authors:** J. Lu, K. Rodenburg, L. Foti, & A. Pegoraro  
- **Journal:** Business Strategy and the Environment, 31(7) (2022)  
- **Link:** [https://doi.org/10.1002/bse.3088](https://doi.org/10.1002/bse.3088)  
- **Ranking / Impact:** ABDC A; Impact factor 13.3

## In plain language

In this project, we ask whether companies that perform better on sustainability are also better able to cope with crises.  
We look at firmsâ€™ sustainability performance before crises and compare how they fare during disruptive periods, focusing on indicators of financial resilience.  
Our findings suggest that companies with stronger sustainability performance tend to be more resilient when facing crises, supporting the idea that sustainability can contribute to long-term stability and risk management.
